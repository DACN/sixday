Programme Trail O

Trail-O Event

Venue: Drummond Castle, 4 miles south of Crieff off A822. Parking: In a field just beyond Drummond Castle. Organiser: Anne Hickling (ESOC). Controller: Rob Hickling (BASOC). Planner: Roger Scrutton (ESOC) Start times: 12:00-14:00. No allocation of start times. Map: 1:4,000, 2011 map by Stirling Surveys, updated for TrailO by Rob Hickling, May 2019. Courses: One course only, with 5 TempO and 8 PreO stations (subject to final controlling). The course will be along good tracks, suitable for wheelchairs. EOD: Subject to map availability. Elite: Senior £10, Junior/Student £4.
